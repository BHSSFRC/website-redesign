Welcome to the home of FRC team 3494, The Quadrangles!




Team 3494, The Quadrangles, is a FIRST Robotics Competition team based out of Bloomington High School South in Bloomington, Indiana. Team members build a robot that competes in national tournaments while gaining real-life engineering experience and learning more about STEM and its applications. Team 3494 was founded in 2011.



For more information about FIRST and FIRST Robotics Competition visit: firstinspires.org.

For more information about FIRST in Indiana, visit: indianafirst.org.
